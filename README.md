# Customer Churn Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

Customer Churn Analysis is a data analytics project developed using **Python**, **SQLite**, and **Jupyter Notebook**. The project focuses on identifying customers who have left or are likely to leave a business by analyzing customer behavior, service usage, and account information.

The primary objective is to help businesses understand the key factors influencing customer churn and support data-driven strategies for improving customer retention.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze customer data to identify churn patterns.
- Understand customer behavior before they leave.
- Explore relationships between customer demographics, services, and churn.
- Generate meaningful visualizations for business insights.
- Store and retrieve customer data efficiently using SQLite.

---
